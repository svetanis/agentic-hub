# One-Day Rome Itinerary: Ancient Wonders & Iconic Fountains
- For: Solo Adult
- From: Paris, France
- To: Rome, Italy
- Date: End of May 2026 (e.g., May 30th)

## Recommended Accommodations
As this is a one-day trip, no overnight accommodation is needed. If you prefer a place to freshen up or store luggage, consider a day-use hotel near Termini Station or a luggage storage service.

## Day-to-Day Plan: Rome in a Day
### Morning: Ancient Rome (8:30 AM - 1:00 PM)

- 8:30 AM - 9:00 AM: Arrive at Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO), take the Leonardo Express train to Termini Station (approx. 32 minutes). Store any small luggage at Termini if needed.
- 9:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Take the Metro (Line B) from Termini to Colosseo station.
- 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Colosseum & Roman Forum. Marvel at the iconic Colosseum from the outside (consider booking a skip-the-line ticket in advance if you wish to enter, but for a one-day trip, viewing from outside and exploring the surrounding area might be more time-efficient). Walk through the Roman Forum and Palatine - Hill area, imagining ancient Roman life.
- 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM: Walk towards the Pantheon. Enjoy the impressive architecture of this ancient Roman temple, now a church.

### Lunch & Afternoon: Baroque Beauty (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

- 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Lunch near the Pantheon. Grab a quick, authentic Roman pizza slice or a pasta dish at a local trattoria.
- 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Walk to the Trevi Fountain. Toss a coin over your shoulder to ensure your return to Rome! Be prepared for crowds.
- 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Stroll to the Spanish Steps (Piazza di Spagna). Climb the steps for a view, or simply enjoy the atmosphere at the base.
- 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Wander through Piazza Navona, admiring Bernini's Fountain of Four Rivers and the lively street artists. Enjoy a gelato!

### Late Afternoon/Evening: Last Bites & Departure (5:00 PM - 9:00 PM)

- 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM: Enjoy some free time for souvenir shopping or a final coffee/aperitivo near Piazza Navona or Campo de' Fiori.
- 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM: Early Dinner. Indulge in a final Roman meal – perhaps Cacio e Pepe or Carbonara – near Termini Station for convenience.
- 7:30 PM - 8:00 PM: Head back to Termini Station.
- 8:00 PM - 8:30 PM: Take the Leonardo Express back to Fiumicino Airport (FCO).
- 9:00 PM: Depart from Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO).
